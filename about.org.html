* About Me

I am scientific software developer living in beautiful Boulder, Colorado USA. I am passionate about the application of computing technology to science and math. I am currently employed at the Unidata Program Center and have been part of the UCAR organization [fn:1] since 1999 when I started here as a student. I specialize in scientific data, metadata, analysis and visualization using a variety of programming languages and technologies.

[fn:1] The Unidata Program Center (UPC) is part of the UCAR Community Programs (UCP), which is part of the University Corporation for Atmospheric Research (UCAR). The most well-known organization within UCAR is the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) partially located at the iconic Mesa Lab in Boulder.
